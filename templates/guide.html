Professional Document Scanning

Scanner Guide

Capture Tips

  • Use camera or upload file
  • Ensure good lighting
  • Keep document within frame
  • Avoid shadows when possible
  • Hold the camera steady
  • Ensure all corners are visible

Best Results

  • Use auto-enhance for most documents
  • Try B&W mode for text documents
  • Enable shadow removal if needed
  • Use sharpen for better text clarity
  • Auto-straighten helps with alignment

Scanner Options

  • Auto Enhance: Automatically improve image quality
  • Remove Shadows: Remove dark areas and shadows
  • Auto-straighten: Correct document alignment
  • Color Mode: Choose between original, enhanced, B&W, or grayscale
  • Sharpen Text: Improve text clarity
  • Reduce Noise: Remove image noise and artifacts
