Complete Scanner Guide

Getting Started

Quick Start Steps
  1. Position your document on a flat surface
  2. Open camera or choose file upload
  3. Align document within frame
  4. Capture or select image
  5. Apply enhancements as needed
Supported Documents
  • Text documents & letters
  • ID cards & certificates
  • Receipts & invoices
  • Forms & contracts
  • Handwritten notes

Scanner Features

Color Modes

Original: Keeps original colors
Enhanced: Improves color balance and contrast
B&W: Best for text documents
Grayscale: Good for photos and detailed documents

Enhancement Options

Auto-straighten: Corrects document alignment
Clean Background: Removes shadows and improves contrast
Sharpen Text: Enhances text clarity and readability

Best Practices

  • Lighting: Use even, bright lighting. Avoid harsh shadows.
  • Positioning: Ensure all corners are visible and document fills the frame.
  • Stability: Keep device steady while capturing.
  • Contrast: Ensure good contrast between document and background.

Troubleshooting

  • Hold device steady while capturing
  • Ensure adequate lighting
  • Clean camera lens
  • Try using the sharpen feature

  • Try B&W mode for text documents
  • Enable text sharpening
  • Adjust contrast settings
  • Ensure proper lighting
